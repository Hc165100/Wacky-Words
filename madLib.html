Movie Pitch
So here's the pitch. A 20 year old woman decides to start a new life in Korea. For 12 years they have studied the art of painting, and are finally leaving their teacher to make their own paintings. Before they can rent a car to travel away, a green dog comes to them in a dream and says "Gosh! We still need you to find the black hammer. The fate of all Italy depends on it." This dream makes them feel strange, but they decide to start looking after a good night's rest. What follows is a story of intrigue, spies, and delicious pizza. What do you think?

Behold the tale of Captain fuzzy Legs! He is the most fearsome buccaneer on the 7 seas. On board his ship Robert's Dog, he and his crew search for all the frogs they can steal. You can always see the Captain coming on the horizon, his green flag emblazoned with a skull-faced turtle flapping in the wind. In spite of all his fierceness, he has one great weakness: his fear of the giant red lobster. It has haunted the captain's dreams for 12 years, ever since it killed his kittens. He always has his trusty sword at hand, in case he should ever meet it again.

Let me tell you about the strangest date I ever had. It was the 2nd time we went out, and we decided to go to this Greek restaurant. The problem was, when we got there, we didn't realize it was taco night. There was a large group of 21 orchids celebrating, taking up half the place. Nevertheless our waiter Jessica came to our table, and we ordered some orange juice and two dinner specials. While we waited for our food, my date decided to tell me about the wonderful world of stamps, which they had collected since they were 6. After what seemed like years, our order came out, but it was totally wrong! The food was completely dry, and the waiter spilled our drinks all over my skirt. That was the last straw. My date tried to stop me, but I just said, "Kiss my toe!" and walked out; never saw them again.
